What sauce do I make?

10 bowls of mystery liquid. 3 foolproof combos.

🥜 Classic Sesame (safe, nutty)

Sesame paste 芝麻酱 + garlic water 蒜水 + vinegar 醋 + cilantro

→ Best with: beef, tofu, noodles, cabbage

🌶️ Spicy Garlic (hot, bold)

Chili oil 辣椒油 + soy sauce 酱油 + minced garlic 蒜泥 + scallions

→ Best with: tripe, mushrooms, lotus root

🧂 Light & Clean (mild, easy)

Soy sauce 酱油 + vinegar 醋 + sesame oil 香油 + scallions

→ Best with: veggies, shrimp, fish balls

Anything else I should know?

  • 1 Can I drink the broth? Yes — unless you ordered 特辣. Your stomach may disagree.
  • 2 Fill your bowl to 70%. It expands when cooked.
  • 3 Grab chopsticks AND a spoon. You'll want both.
  • 4 Say 微辣 (wēi là) for mild. Say 好吃 (hǎo chī) when done — guaranteed smile.
  • 5 First time is overwhelming. Fifth time is life-changing. You got this.
