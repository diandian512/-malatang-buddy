More Tips for First-Timers

  • 1 Can I drink the broth? Yes — unless you ordered 特辣.
  • 2 Fill your bowl to 70%. It expands when cooked.
  • 3 Grab chopsticks AND a spoon. You'll need both.
  • 4 Say 微辣 (wēi là) for mild spicy. Say 好吃 (hǎo chī) when you're done.
  • 5 First time is overwhelming. Fifth time is life-changing.

Frequently Asked Questions About Malatang

What is malatang? Malatang is a Chinese spicy soup dish where you pick your own ingredients and pay by weight.

How do you order malatang? Grab a bowl, pick ingredients from the fridge, hand it to the cashier, choose your spice level, and wait 3 minutes.

How much does malatang cost? A normal bowl costs $3-6 depending on what you pick. Price is by weight.

Is malatang spicy? You can choose your spice level from not spicy to extra spicy. Beginners should start with 微辣 (mild).

What does malatang taste like? It's a savory, spicy, numbing broth with fresh ingredients. The numbing comes from Sichuan peppercorn.

Can vegetarians eat malatang? Yes. Choose tomato or mushroom broth and pick vegetables, tofu, and glass noodles.

Is malatang gluten free? Most ingredients are naturally gluten free. Avoid instant noodles and luncheon meat.

Is malatang halal? Some Yangguofu locations are halal-certified. Ask for chicken or lamb instead of pork.

What sauce goes with malatang? Try the classic sesame combo, spicy garlic combo, or light & clean combo.

What ingredients are in malatang? Beef, lamb, chicken, fish balls, tofu, mushrooms, vegetables, noodles, and more.
