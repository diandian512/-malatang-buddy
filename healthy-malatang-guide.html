⚡ The Golden Rules

What influencers, nutritionists, and real people say about healthy Málà Tāng

✅ Load Up On

  • Leafy greens & mushrooms
  • Lean beef & chicken breast
  • Tofu, quail eggs, duck blood
  • Konjac noodles (almost zero cal)
  • Corn, lotus root (smart carbs)

❌ Avoid These

  • Fried meatballs (oil sponges)
  • Instant noodles & fried dough
  • Creamy white broth (pure fat)
  • Sesame paste (1 tbsp ≈ 100 kcal)
  • Drinking the broth (salt + purine)

💡 Pro Tips From Top Posts

🥗
1:2:1 Ratio — Protein : Vegetables : Carbs. Build your bowl around this.
🕐
Eat it at lunch — Málà Tāng is best enjoyed midday. Behave yourself at dinner.
🥤
Never drink the broth — It's loaded with oil, salt, and purine. Just a taste is enough.
💧
Drink water after — Prevents water retention and helps flush out excess sodium.
🍜
Skip processed meatballs — They're mostly starch and additives, not real protein.
🌶️
Add spice separately — Use fresh chili or chili flakes instead of relying on the oily broth.
